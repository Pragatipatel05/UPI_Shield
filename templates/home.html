Common Fraud Techniques

Fake Payment Screenshots

Doctored images showing fake payments to deceive merchants and individuals.

"Friend in Need" Scams

Impersonating relatives or friends to request urgent financial help.

Malicious QR Codes

QR codes that debit money from accounts instead of crediting as expected.

Screen Monitoring Apps

Malicious applications that record screen activity to steal PINs and passwords.

Collect Request Frauds

Fake refund requests that trick users into paying money instead of receiving it.

Vishing Attacks

Voice calls impersonating bank officials to extract sensitive information.

Top Frauds in India

KYC Update Scam

Tricking users to share KYC details through fake notifications.

Fake Customer Care

Scammers posing as bank helplines to steal credentials.

Remote Access Apps

Attackers gain control via apps like AnyDesk or TeamViewer.

Lottery Offers

False promises of prizes or cashback to lure victims.

UPI Fraud Recovery Process

  1. Immediate Action: Report the transaction in your UPI app immediately.
  2. Contact Bank: Reach out to your bank with complete transaction details.
  3. Cybercrime Helpline: Call 1930 or visit cybercrime.gov.in.
  4. File FIR: Visit the nearest cybercrime police station to file an FIR.
  5. Escalation: If unresolved in 30 days, escalate to RBI Ombudsman.
